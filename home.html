Kampala Air Quality Prediction

Introduction

Welcome to the official site of the Kampala Air-Predict (KAP) app! Our innovative project aims to provide real-time air quality predictions for various parts of Kampala using the power of Image Analysis and Machine Learning. By simply uploading a picture or taking a picture using a camera, you can instantly get an accurate Air Quality Index (AQI) reading for your location. Our mission is to improve public health, reduce economic costs, and raise environmental awareness in Kampala.

Overview

Kampala Air-Predict is designed to make air quality monitoring accessible and convenient for everyone. Using advanced Computer Vision and Machine Learning technologies, our web application analyzes images to predict air quality levels. Trained on hundreds of images from around Kampala, our model delivers reliable predictions, helping you make informed decisions about your daily activities to minimize exposure to air pollution.

Our Technology

Kampala Air-Predict uses a robust client-server architecture to deliver its services. The front end of our web application is built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript within the Django framework, ensuring a responsive and interactive user experience. The backend processes images and delivers AQI predictions using a sophisticated machine learning model. This architecture not only separates the user interface from the backend services but also distributes processing loads efficiently, ensuring scalability and reliability.

Data and Privacy

Your privacy is our priority. The images you upload and your personal data are securely stored in our database. Only authenticated users can access the system, ensuring your data is protected. The database holds user authentication details, image data, and prediction results, which are used solely for providing you with accurate air quality information.
